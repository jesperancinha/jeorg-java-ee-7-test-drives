Using Locks to plant a plant!!

  • In this page submit a plant
  • In another page submit another plant
  • In yet another page just open this
  • You should see that the last write operation will hang for about 20 seconds (you have to open one after the other)
  • The read operation should take just as long
  • Now do the same thing but in this case, just open 2 pages
  • You should now see that they lastonly 10 seconds
  • This proves that the write operation block everything, and the read operation always happens.
